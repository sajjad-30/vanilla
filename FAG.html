Frequently Asked Questions

An FAQ page is a list of frequently asked questions and their corresponding answers. It helps provide quick information about a product or service.

The accordion is a collapsible menu where one section expands at a time when clicked, showing the content inside. It saves space and is easy to navigate.

This FAQ page is useful because it provides concise answers to common questions, allowing users to quickly find the information they need without searching through the whole site.

To add more FAQs, simply copy one of the FAQ sections and modify the question and answer to suit your needs.
